slide

	a.)slideDown(speed, callbackFunction):-used to slide down an element.

	b.)slideUp(speed, callbackFunction):-used to slide up an element.

	c.)slideToggle(speed, callbackFunction):-
		If the elements have been slid down, slideToggle() will slide them up.
		If the elements have been slid up, slideToggle() will slide them down.
